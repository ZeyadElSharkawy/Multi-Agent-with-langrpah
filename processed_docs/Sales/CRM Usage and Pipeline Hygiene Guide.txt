1. Data Integrity Mandate 
The CRM is the single source of truth for all customer interactions, forecast data, and pipeline 
metrics. Inaccurate data directly impacts commissions, forecasting, and company 
strategy.

4. Pipeline Hygiene Rules 
●​ Opportunities dormant for 45 days without a "Next Step" update will be automatically 
flagged for review by Sales Operations and may be moved to the "Stalled" pipeline. 
●​ All customer-facing documents (Proposals, SOWs) must be uploaded to the 
corresponding Opportunity file.
