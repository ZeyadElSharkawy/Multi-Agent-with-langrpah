3. Preventative Measures 
●​ Dust Control: Server room and network closets must be vacuumed and cleaned weekly 
by the facilities team. 
●​ Physical Security: Ensure all network closets remain locked at all times. Keys are held 
exclusively by the Facility Manager and Infrastructure Engineer.
