1. Compensation Philosophy 
NexaCore Solutions is committed to a Total Rewards strategy that attracts and retains top 
talent in the competitive SaaS industry across the MENA region. Our compensation is 
structured to be competitive with market benchmarks, reward high performance, and align 
employees with the company's long-term success.
