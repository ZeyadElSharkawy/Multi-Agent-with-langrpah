1.​ KSA PoP: IT to confirm technical viability and financial feasibility of a physical 
Point-of-Presence in KSA by Q2 2026. 
2.​ Contract Localization: Legal to create jurisdiction-specific contract addenda for all new 
KSA and Egypt enterprise contracts. 
3.​ Training: Roll out mandatory training for Sales and Legal teams on regional data 
handling requirements.
