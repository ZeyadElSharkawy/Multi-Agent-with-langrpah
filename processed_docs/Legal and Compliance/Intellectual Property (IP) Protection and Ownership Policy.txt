1. Ownership of Developed IP 
NexaCore Solutions is a technology company whose core value lies in its Intellectual Property 
(IP). 
●​ Work Product: All inventions, discoveries, software code, algorithms, machine learning 
models, training data sets, technical documentation, and business processes developed 
or created by an Employee during the course of employment shall be deemed "Work 
Product". 
●​ Assignment: All rights, title, and interest in and to the Work Product are the exclusive 
property of NexaCore Solutions, with no additional consideration due to the Employee 
beyond their regular salary. This is confirmed via the standard Employee Confidentiality 
and IP Assignment Agreement.

3. Third-Party IP and Open Source Software (OSS) 
●​ Use of Third-Party Materials: No external software, code snippets, licensed APIs, or 
data sets may be incorporated into NexaCore’s products without prior written approval 
from the Engineering Lead and Legal Department, confirming license compatibility. 
●​ Open Source Governance: The use of Open Source Software (OSS) must comply with 
the Software Licensing and Open Source Policy. Developers must use an approved 
software composition analysis (SCA) tool to track and manage all OSS dependencies and 
licenses. 
4. Enforcement 
The Legal Department is mandated to actively monitor and pursue all necessary legal action 
against any infringement, misappropriation, or unauthorized disclosure of NexaCore’s IP.
