1. Purpose 
This summary outlines the core terms of NexaCore's standard Mutual and Unilateral 
Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs), used to protect Confidential Information (CI) when 
exploring partnerships, vendor relationships, or major client pre-sales discussions.
