Q1 2026 includes the General Availability (GA) launch of the Predictive AI Workflow Trigger 
(PAWT) and increased focus on the KSA regional market. The goal is to allocate specialized 
L2 resources to manage the expected increase in complex AI and regional compliance tickets.
