3. Exclusions from Downtime Calculation 
The following are not counted as service downtime: 
1.​ Scheduled Maintenance (pre-notified at least 48 hours in advance). 
2.​ Factors outside of NexaCore's reasonable control (Force Majeure, client's network 
issues). 
3.​ Client's use of non-approved third-party software or configurations.
