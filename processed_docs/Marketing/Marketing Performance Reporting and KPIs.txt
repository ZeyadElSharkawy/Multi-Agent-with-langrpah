3. Reporting Cadence 
●​ Weekly Dashboard Review: Focus on MQL volume, MQL-to-SQL conversion rate, and 
pipeline velocity. 
●​ Monthly Performance Review: Comprehensive review of all KPIs, CAC, and channel 
spend efficiency. 
●​ Quarterly Business Review (QBR): Deep dive into strategic performance (Brand 
Awareness, CLV analysis, Budget Variance). 
4. Attribution Model 
NexaCore uses a Weighted Multi-Touch Attribution model, giving partial credit to all 
marketing touches (first touch, mid-touch, and final touch) that occurred before the lead 
converted to a Sales Accepted Lead (SAL).
